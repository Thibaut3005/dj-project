About DJ Angerone

Mike Myers, professionally known as DJ Angerone, is internationally acclaimed progressive house & Trance DJ & musician.

I try to experiment with various music genre in every track that i make

Since the begining of his career in 2010, he has already released 5 EPs on a range of worlwide known labels (Booster Records, DSTRCT Recording etc.) and received a number of music awards.
